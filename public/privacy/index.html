Privacy Policy

Introduction

At WBHRMS, we prioritize the privacy of our users. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and safeguard your information while using our HRMS platform, designed specifically for the Wedding Banquets company.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information to deliver our services effectively:

  • Personal Information: Employee names, contact details, job titles, addresses, and salaries.
  • Attendance and Leave Records: Data related to employee check-ins, check-outs, and leave management.
  • Payroll Data: Salary calculations, deductions, and payment reports.
  • Performance Data: Employee task assignments, completion statuses, and evaluations.
  • System Usage: Logs, device details, and IP addresses for security and analytics.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used for the following purposes:

  • Managing employee profiles, roles, and permissions.
  • Tracking attendance and leave applications efficiently.
  • Automating payroll processes for accuracy and timeliness.
  • Evaluating employee performance and productivity.
  • Enhancing security and improving system performance through analytics.

3. How We Share Your Information

We only share your information in the following scenarios:

  • Within the Organization: HR and management teams access relevant data for operational purposes.
  • Third-Party Services: Integration with external payroll or analytics services (only with your consent).
  • Legal Obligations: If required by law or legal processes.

4. Data Security

We implement robust security measures to protect your data:

  • Encryption for sensitive data storage and transmission.
  • Secure access controls to limit unauthorized access.
  • Regular audits and vulnerability assessments.

While we strive to safeguard your information, no method of data transmission is 100% secure.

5. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access and update your personal information.
  • Request the deletion of your data where applicable.
  • Withdraw consent for data processing at any time.

To exercise your rights, contact us at it@weddingbanquets.in.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy or as required by law. Upon request, we will securely delete or anonymize your data when it is no longer needed.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be communicated via the platform or email. Your continued use of WBHRMS after updates indicates your acceptance of the revised policy.
